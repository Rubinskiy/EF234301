Bukittinggi is the third largest city in West Sumatra, Indonesia. It is in the Minangkabau Highlands, 90 km by road from the West Sumatran capital city of Padang. Bukittinggi used to be known as Fort de Kock and was once dubbed 'Parijs van Sumatera'. It also used to be a centre of government at the time of the Dutch East Indies.
